True Worship Sessions

True Worship Sessions is a life-transforming monthly worship programme hosted by me, Laide Endeley. The purpose of this interdenominational programme is to restore and rekindle fellowship between God the Father and His children, through "true worship" as our motto is "Worship = Fellowship" (John 4:24). We had our first session on Sunday December 25, 2022 and we've been running since then. Volunteers and Partners are warmly welcome!

True Worship Sessions, Volume 1

True Worship Sessions Volume 1 took place at MFM Region 10, Utako - Abuja on Sunday 25th December, 2022. The theme was "Celebrating Jesus." It was an awesome time leading worship with Ministers Lamide Endeley, Peace Abah, Dejaks and Danaki. The Worship Clan also ministered and it was such a blessing. Special thanks to our excellent instrumentalists led by Faadarjosh. We all had a wonderful time celebrating Jesus.

True Worship Sessions, Volume 2

True Worship Sessions Volume 1 took place at Abba Music Studio, Apo - Abuja on Sunday 29th January, 2023. The theme was "Agape Love." It was an beautiful time leading worship with Ministers Lamide Endeley, Pleasant Olanrewaju and the amazing Backing Vocalists: Ekama, Charis and Prince. The instrumentalists were excellent as usual and led by Faadarjosh. Finally, MUM was present and she attested to having the BEST time! If we felt anything this day, it was God's love.
